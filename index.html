South Park - Eric Cartman

About

Eric Theodore Cartman, usually referred to as just Cartman, is one of the main characters, along with Stan Marsh, Kyle Broflovski, and Kenny McCormick. Cartman is sometimes the main antagonist of an episode. None of the other three main children really consider Cartman their friend and do not know why they started hanging out with him in the first place, but a certain form of unstable friendship ends up being born between them.

Accomplishments

Over the course of the series, Cartman managed to accomplish a wide range of achievements. Here are just some of them.

Buying a theme park

Cartman bought a theme park with one million dollars he inherited from his grandmother. After selling it back to the original owner, he blew all his money.

Forming a christian rock band Faith + 1

The group's self-titled debut album, Faith + 1, was a massive success, quickly selling over one million copies.

Becoming a NASCAR driver

Cartman enters and almost wins an official NASCAR race, but ultimately fails to secure a win. Altough he suceeds in causing a lot of crashes.

Being a professional food critic

Cartman becomes a "professional" food critic. Using the app Yelp! he goes around the town reviewing food and attempting to get free food.

Talents

Cartman is often shown to be a poor student, but excels in leadership and team organization which usually involves exploitation of others' weaknesses.

Strategist, organizer and speaker.

Cartman devises elaborate plans to achieve his goals, sometimes presenting them to his friends using a whiteboard.

Multilingualism

Cartman is the only one of the children in South Park to speak Spanish fluently. Cartman also speaks German, but most of his words are mispronounced.

Ventriloquism

Cartman has been shown to be an expert in the act of ventriloquism. It is also shown that Cartman sometimes uses his talent to express his delusional side, he demonstrates this through his hand, which he calls Mitch Conner.

Music

Cartman is quite musically talented. He can play many instruments (some better than others) such as: the guitar, the piano, the violin, the French horn, the drums, the harmonica, the recorder flute and the pan flute. He is also shown to be quite a good singer on multiple occasions.

Family

Cartman's extended family is shown in the episode "Merry Christmas Charlie Manson!", where he and the others go on a trip to Nebraska to visit them for Christmas. Most of his relatives appear to be similar in manner to him; all fat, with the same speech accent and catchphrases.

The most active family member in Cartman's life is his mother, Liane Cartman. Liane dotes on her son endlessly, beyond simply spoiling him. Cartman usually gets whatever he wants simply by whining in his signature style. He also frequently insults her in her presence, which she accepts without question.
